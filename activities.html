Activities

Through the years, the annual activities of the association have become multifaceted, consisting of various programs and projects that support professional librarianship and the promotion of reading culture.

a) Awarding of the Outstanding Professional Librarians Award given annually by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) in June. PLAI handles the nomination process. Other awards granted every year include:

• Hall of Fame Award – the highest award given to a member in recognition of professional excellence, leadership, and service.
• Eulogio B. Rodriguez Professional Award – given in recognition of remarkable professionalism or major contribution to Philippine librarianship.
• Gabriel A. Bernardo Leadership Award – given to the immediate past president for meritorious service.
• Cirilio B. Perez Award – given in appreciation of leadership in organizing the previous year’s National Book Week celebration.
• Severino I. Velasco Award – given to the overall chairperson of the past year’s National Conference Committee.
• Service Award – given in gratitude for services rendered by any past officer, trustee, or member for at least four years.

b) Publication of library literature such as regular quarterly newsletters, annual directories of members, bulletins, professional journals, proceedings, and similar materials.

c) Granting of scholarships as financial assistance to members for graduate thesis work in the amount of ₱5,000 each. In the year 2000, the association also awarded ₱10,000 for the best thesis or graduate research work, and another ₱10,000 for the best librarian’s research work.

d) Support for international librarianship through membership and active participation in regional and international organizations such as the International Federation of Library Associations (IFLA) and the Congress of Southeast Asian Librarians (CONSAL).

e) Advocacy programs promoting the interests of professional librarians through lobbying, mass demonstrations, walks-for-a-cause, fund drives, and related activities that strengthen unity among members.

f) Outreach programs including storytelling for orphaned children, book donations to barangay libraries and public information centers, librarians’ visits to prisons, and other initiatives aimed at promoting reading habits nationwide, especially during National Book Week.

g) Participation in the annual Philippine Book Fair and other book fairs at the local, regional, and international levels.

h) Holding of annual fellowship luncheons, anniversary celebrations, and related events that foster camaraderie among members.
